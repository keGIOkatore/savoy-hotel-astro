Suspended between the blue of the sky and the golden sands of Bibione, the Sky Beach Terrace is our exclusive stage by the sea. 

A contemporary *al fresco* lounge where the sea breeze meets high mixology. Perfect for a sunset aperitif accompanied by sophisticated finger food, or for an unforgettable evening under the stars. Here, time slows down, and every detail is designed to celebrate the beauty of the horizon in absolute privacy.
